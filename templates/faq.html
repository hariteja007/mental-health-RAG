Frequently Asked Questions

About the Chatbot

  • Q: What can this chatbot help me with?
    A: The chatbot provides information on mental health topics, self-care tips, coping strategies, and resources. It’s designed to guide you with knowledge and tools for improving your mental well-being but is not a substitute for professional therapy.
  • Q: Is this chatbot a replacement for a therapist?
    A: No, this chatbot is for informational purposes only. For diagnosis, treatment, or therapy, please consult a licensed mental health professional.
  • Q: Is my conversation confidential?
    A: Yes, your privacy is a priority. Conversations with the chatbot are secure and not shared unless explicitly mentioned in the terms of use.
  • Q: How do I ask questions or seek help using this chatbot?
    A: Simply type your question or describe your concern, and the chatbot will provide relevant resources, tips, or links to further information.

General Mental Health Questions

  • Q: What are some common signs of stress or anxiety?
    A: Symptoms can include restlessness, difficulty concentrating, irritability, trouble sleeping, rapid heartbeat, or feeling overwhelmed. These may vary from person to person.
  • Q: How can I manage stress in daily life?
    A: Regular exercise, deep breathing, mindfulness, prioritizing tasks, and maintaining a balanced diet are effective strategies for managing stress.
  • Q: When should I seek professional help for mental health issues?
    A: If you experience persistent sadness, anxiety, or stress that interferes with daily life, or have thoughts of self-harm, seek help from a mental health professional immediately.
  • Q: Are there any free mental health resources in India?
    A: Yes, several organizations provide free or affordable mental health support, such as iCall, Vandrevala Foundation, and AASRA. You can also access helplines for immediate assistance.
  • Q: How can I help a friend struggling with mental health?
    A: Be supportive, listen without judgment, and encourage them to seek professional help. Avoid offering solutions unless you’re a trained expert.
