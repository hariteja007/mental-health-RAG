What to Do in a Crisis

  • Stay Calm: Take slow, deep breaths and focus on staying composed.
  • Contact Help: Call a trusted friend, family member, or a mental health hotline.
  • Visit a Professional: If safe, visit a hospital or mental health center for immediate assistance.
  • Call Emergency Services: In life-threatening situations, call 112 or local emergency services for urgent help.
